Research

I am interested in understanding the fundamental degrees of freedom present in M-theory and how we can use string theory constructions to explore them. The discovery of a theory by Bagger and Lambert of multiple M2-branes heralded the start of a revolution in our understanding of M-theory. The current holy grail is a theory of multiple M5-branes. However, this is a formidable challenge. I hope to gain insight into multiple M5-branes by exploring the degrees of freedom on D4-branes, particularly those associated with instantons.
